Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

Data Collection How We Use Data Sharing Your Rights Account Deletion

VeloFriend ("we," "us," or "our") operates the VeloFriend mobile application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

VeloFriend is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. We process your fitness data locally on your device whenever possible and never sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and nickname provided during registration via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Information: Physical attributes such as height, weight, and sex, which are used to improve the accuracy of our fitness algorithms and Velo-Gotchi mechanics.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your precise location (GPS) to show you nearby riders on the map. You can disable this at any time in the app settings.

Fitness & Health Data (Health Connect)

Our app uses Google Health Connect to access health and fitness data stored on your device. We access the following data types:

  • Cycling Activities: We read recorded cycling sessions.
  • Distance: Total distance covered during activities.
  • Duration: Time elapsed during cycling activities.

Data Flow: All health data is accessed via Health Connect and remains on your device unless explicitly shared by you through social features. We do not share your health data with third parties.

User Control: You can revoke access at any time through the Health Connect app settings on your device.

Manual Entries

Data you manually input, such as ride duration, distance, and perceived effort, is used to supplement your fitness profile.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • Gamification: To calculate your Velo-Gotchi's health, happiness, and experience points.
  • Activity Display: To display your cycling activity and progress within the app.
  • AI Insights: To provide insights and classify your "Athlete Type" using on-device machine learning based on your recent activity trends.
  • Social Discovery: To allow you to find and connect with other cyclists in your immediate vicinity.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Community Sharing

By default, your specific fitness metrics (distance, speed, health data) are private. If you choose to participate in social features, other users may see your nickname, Athlete Type badge, and general location (if enabled). Your raw health data is never shared with third parties.

Service Providers

We use third-party Service Providers to facilitate our Service:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage (Firestore), authentication, and hosting. Data is stored securely in Google Cloud regions.

4. GDPR and International Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. VeloFriend aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data.

  • Access & Portability: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You have the right to have your information rectified if it is inaccurate.
  • Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of your Personal Data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. You can delete your account at any time within the App settings. Upon deletion:

  • All profile data, fitness records, and location history will be permanently removed from our production databases.
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 30 days before being fully purged.

To request manual data deletion, please contact us at contact@getvelofriend.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: contact@getvelofriend.com
