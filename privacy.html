Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy: VeloFriend is built on the principle of "Privacy by Choice." We believe your data should serve your personal experience first. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not disclose your raw Strava metrics to other users.

Welcome to VeloFriend! We are committed to protecting your privacy while fostering a fun, social cycling community. This policy explains how we handle your data in the context of our social and gamified features.

How We Handle Strava Data

VeloFriend connects to your Strava account to power your personal Velo-Gotchi and calculate your Athlete Archetype. To protect your privacy:

  • No Raw Disclosure: We never share your raw Strava data (such as exact distance, speed, heart rate, or GPS paths) with other users.
  • Subjective Labels: We only disclose high-level, subjective labels (e.g., "Endurance Goat", "Climber") if you choose to share them.
  • Private by Default: All social sharing features are disabled by default. You are in total control of what the community sees.

Velo-Gotchi & Gamification

We process your ride activity (Strava or Manual) to maintain your Velo-Gotchi’s health and levels. This data is part of your private profile and is only used to enhance your personal gaming experience within the app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, nickname, and email for account security.
  • Location Data: Collected only when using the map to help you find nearby "VeloFriends."
  • Manual Ride Data: If you prefer not to use Strava, you can log rides manually to feed your Velo-Gotchi.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can manage your privacy settings directly in the app. To delete your account and all associated data (including Strava sync history), please contact us at contact@getvelofriend.com. We will permanently remove all your data from our Firebase servers within 30 days.

Terms of Service

By using VeloFriend, you agree to:

  • Respect the Pack: Harassment or offensive language will lead to immediate account termination.
  • Ride Safe: VeloFriend is for social discovery. Always follow local traffic laws and prioritize safety over gamified goals.
  • Fair Play: Do not attempt to "spoof" data to manipulate Legend rankings or Velo-Gotchi levels.
