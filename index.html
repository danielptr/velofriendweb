The Athlete Profile

VeloFriend discovers your unique cycling personality. Our algorithm analyzes your patterns to determine your "Athlete Type" across four key dimensions:

🚀
Endurance Your ability to go the distance and keep the wheels turning.
🔥
Intensity Speed and push on the flats. Calculated from your best efforts.
👑
Consistency How regularly you show up, week after week.
⛰️
Ascender Your mastery of elevation gain and love for the hills.
