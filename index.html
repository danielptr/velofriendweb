The Athlete Profile

VeloFriend doesn't just track miles; it discovers your unique cycling personality. Our algorithm analyzes your ride patterns to determine your "Athlete Type" across five key dimensions:

🚀
Endurance Your long-distance capability and overall stamina.
🔥
Intensity Speed, average effort, and your push on the flats.
👑
Consistency How regularly you show up and keep the wheels turning.
⛰️
Ascender Your mastery and frequency of elevation gain.
📅
Weekly Volume Your total time and effort spent in the saddle each week.
